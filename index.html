    Secdea Beach Resort

    pronounced "sek-deh-yah"

    Secdea Beach Resort offers a beautiful beach setting, modern amenities,
    and unique architecture.
    Located in San Isidro, Babac, the resort is known for its Infinity Restaurant,
    which has stunning views of the infinity pool.
    The Mangrove Area and wooden bridge are perfect for leisurely walks.
    Although the costs may be high, the experience is worth it.
